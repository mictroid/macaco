## 2. Information we collect

We only collect what the App needs to work. Specifically:

### a) Account information
When you sign in, we collect your **email address** and a unique **account identifier**
through your chosen sign-in method (Google, Apple, or email/password). If you set a
profile name or photo, we store those too.

### b) Your journal content
The entries you create are stored in your account and may include:
- Titles, locations, dates, and written descriptions
- A mood indicator and tags you add
- **Photos** you attach (from your gallery or taken with the camera)

This content is private to your account. We do not read, analyse, or share it.

### c) Purchase information
If you buy a premium subscription, the purchase is processed by **Google Play Billing**
and managed through **RevenueCat**. We receive your subscription status (active or not)
and a purchase identifier. **We never receive or store your payment card details** —
those are handled entirely by Google.

### d) Photos and camera
With your permission, the App accesses your **photo library** and **camera** so you can
attach images to entries. Photos stay associated with your entries; they are not used for
any other purpose.

### e) Google Drive (optional)
If you connect Google Drive, the App backs up your entry photos to a folder named
"Macaco" in **your own** Google Drive. We access only the files the App creates
(the `drive.file` scope). We cannot see the rest of your Drive.

### f) App preferences
Settings such as theme, language, and whether App Lock is enabled are stored **locally on
your device** and are not transmitted to us.

### g) Information we do **not** collect
We do not collect advertising identifiers, location tracking data, contacts, or analytics
profiles beyond what the services below require to function.

## 5. Where your data is stored

Your account and journal entries are stored on **Google Firebase** servers, which may be
located in countries other than your own. These providers maintain industry-standard
security measures. Where data is transferred internationally, it is protected by the
safeguards offered by those providers (such as Standard Contractual Clauses).

## 6. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account or ask
us to delete your data, we remove your entries and account information from our active
systems. Backups stored in **your own** Google Drive remain under your control and must be
deleted by you.

## 7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the EU/UK **GDPR** or the
**California CCPA/CPRA**), you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Export a copy of your data
- Object to or restrict certain processing

You can exercise several of these directly in the App: edit or delete entries at any time,
and use **Settings → Backup & Restore** to export all your data to a file. For account
deletion or any other request, email us at **houseofmmminq@gmail.com** and we will respond within
the time required by applicable law.

information.
