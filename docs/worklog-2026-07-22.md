### `code-brief-drive-download-race.md` — serialize overlapping Drive download passes

Large first-time syncs (200+ photos, no local cache) could silently fail to load most photos while
the banner undercounts failures: the Firestore entries listener emits more than once during initial
load and each emission spawned an independent `downloadMissing()` pass with its own semaphore,
pushing aggregate concurrent Drive requests past the limit and tripping `403 userRateLimitExceeded`.

- Added `driveDownloadMutex = Mutex()` next to `driveUploadMutex`; wrapped `downloadMissing()`'s
  body in `driveDownloadMutex.withLock { }` (both `return@withContext` → `return@withLock`). A second
  overlapping call now waits and finds most work already in `cachedPhotoUris`. No new imports.
- **Files:** `data/sync/DrivePhotoSync.kt` only.
- **Deviations from brief:** none.
- **Does NOT fix:** genuine 403 retry/backoff on truly enormous libraries (separate follow-up).
