Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

Short version: Macaco stores your journal entries and account info securely in your own cloud account. We don't sell your data, show ads, or share your content with anyone. Your photos go to your own Google Drive — we never hold them on our servers.

1. Who we are

Macaco is a personal travel journal app developed by House of MMMinq. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at houseofmmminq@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

Data Why we collect it Where it's stored
Account info
Name, email address, profile photo		 To identify your account and sync your entries across devices Firebase Authentication (Google)
Journal entries
Title, location, date, description, mood, tags, trip name		 Your travel memories — the core purpose of the app Firebase Firestore, in your personal account subcollection
Photos To attach memories to your entries Your device gallery (MediaStore) and optionally your own Google Drive account
Purchase status To verify your premium subscription Managed by RevenueCat and Google Play — we never handle payment details directly

We do not collect location data automatically, track your usage behaviour, or use analytics SDKs.

3. How we use your data

  • To sync your journal entries across devices you're signed in to
  • To back up and restore your photos via your connected Google Drive account
  • To verify your premium subscription status
  • To send you optional local reminders to write a new entry (no data leaves your device for this)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond running the app for you.

4. Third-party services

Macaco uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Drive API — photos are written to and read from your own Google Drive account. We request the drive.file scope, which limits our access to only files created by Macaco.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — payment processing — policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for their own purposes. The only data transfers that occur are those described above (Firebase sync, your own Drive account, RevenueCat purchase verification).

6. Data retention and deletion

Your journal entries and account data remain in Firebase as long as your account exists. To delete your data:

  • Entries: Delete individual entries from within the app, or delete all entries by signing out and deleting your Firebase account
  • Photos: Removing a photo from an entry deletes it from your device gallery. Drive copies can be deleted from your own Google Drive
  • Account: Contact us at houseofmmminq@gmail.com to request full account deletion

7. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest is protected by Firebase's security rules, which ensure each user can only access their own entries. We do not have access to your Google account credentials — authentication is handled entirely by Google.

8. Children's privacy

Macaco is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to its processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at houseofmmminq@gmail.com.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
