Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Short version: Macaco stores your journal entries and account info securely in your own cloud account. We don't sell your data, show ads, or share your content with anyone. Your photos go to your own Google Drive — we never hold them on our servers.

1. Who we are

Macaco is a personal travel journal app developed by House of Mmminq. Questions? Contact us at houseofmmminq@gmail.com.

By using Macaco you also agree to our Terms of Service.

2. What data we collect

DataWhyWhere stored
Account info
Name, email, profile photo		To identify your account and sync entries across devicesFirebase Authentication (Google)
Journal entries
Title, location, date, description, mood, tags		Your travel memories — the core purpose of the appFirebase Firestore, in your personal subcollection
PhotosTo attach memories to entriesYour device gallery (MediaStore) and optionally your own Google Drive
Purchase statusTo verify your premium subscriptionRevenueCat and Google Play — we never handle payment details directly

We do not collect location data automatically. We use Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) to collect aggregate, anonymised usage data — such as app opens, session length, and feature engagement — to help us improve the app. This data is not linked to your identity. You can opt out via your device's "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" setting. We use Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) to capture crash and stability reports so we can fix problems.

3. How we use your data

  • To sync your journal entries across devices you're signed in to
  • To back up and restore your photos via your connected Google Drive
  • To verify your premium subscription status
  • To send optional local reminders to write a new entry (no data leaves your device for this)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond running the app for you.

4. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Firebase Analytics — aggregate app usage insights — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Drive API — photos read/written to your own Drive. We request only the drive.file scope, limiting access to files created by Macaco.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — payment processing — policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. Data transfers are limited to those described above.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your data remains in Firebase as long as your account exists. To delete:

  • Entries: Delete individually in the app, or delete all via Profile → Delete Account
  • Photos: Removing a photo from an entry deletes it from your device gallery; Drive copies can be deleted from your own Google Drive
  • Account: Profile → Delete Account permanently removes all entries from our servers and deletes your auth record. Photos in your gallery or Drive live in your own accounts and are unaffected.

7. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest is protected by Firebase security rules ensuring each user can only access their own entries. We never access your Google account credentials.

8. Children's privacy

Macaco is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal data and we will delete it.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data, or to object to its processing. Contact us at houseofmmminq@gmail.com.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. We will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

House of Mmminq
